Self check-out

Dear guest,I hope you are doing great and enjoying Budapest and my apartment!


Here is the check-out process:


1. Please turn off the lights and air conditioning if used

2. Wipe the water off the wooden surfaces

3. Wash the dishes or run the dishwasher

4. Leave the keys (fobs & door lock cards) on the white dining table

5. Close the door by entering your 4-digit code (that I sent you via Airbnb app) and press # sign

6. Rotate the lock to the right (clockwise) couple of times to lock the door. After several seconds you’ll hear “buzz” and it means the door is locked (the lock should freely rotate). 

7. I appreciate if you send me a message that you’ve left. Thank you in advance!


The journey to the airport should take about 35 minutes depending on traffic.

If you need to pre-order the taxi to specific time I recommend to use Főtaxi app (available in AppStore and Google Play) or if you don’t need to pre-order you could use Uber/Bolt. 

Have a great day and if you need any help please let me know! Igor
